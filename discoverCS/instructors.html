EECS 110: Meet the Instructor

I'd love to hear from you with any questions that you may have! Please get in touch with me!

ELIZABETH LIPIN    elipin@umich.edu

Hello! I'm Elizabeth Lipin, a first year master's student in CSE. I have a background in both psychology and computer science, and have always been drawn to the intersection between the two fields. I aim to do this by applying AI to game design. I've been work on implementing various AI and ML methods in the card game Durak, and will continue to explore this. In my spare time, I enjoy reading, playing strategy games, and hanging out with my cats.

I have seen the importance of diversity and the representation of under represented groups. I am excited to support you as you take your first steps into the world of computer science and hope you'll gain the same love for computer science as I did. Please feel free to reach out to me at elipin@umich.edu with any questions or even just to chat!

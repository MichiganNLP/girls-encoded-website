Rada Mihalcea

Rada is a professor of computer science and engineering, as well as co-director of the Girls Encoded initiative. Her research interests include natural language processing, multimodal processing, and computational social sciences.

Laura Wendlandt

Laura is a current PhD student in computer science and engineering at the University of Michigan, as well as co-director of the Girls Encoded initiative. Her research explores ways that computers can better understand language. In her spare time, Laura enjoys running, crocheting, and reading novels.

EECS 110: Meet the Instructor

I'd love to hear from you with any questions that you may have! Please get in touch with me!

KATHERINE MAYO    kamayo@umich.edu

Hello! I'm Katherine Mayo, a 4th year PhD student in CSE working in artificial intelligence. I've always been drawn to the interdisciplinary applications of Computer Science. My current research applies techniques from Computer Science and Economics to analyze strategic interactions between banks in financial networks. And prior to graduate school, I worked on projects applying machine learning methods to problems in medicine and national security. In my spare time, I enjoy photography, running, and building mechanical keyboards.

My own experiences as a computer scientist have shown me the importance of diversity and supporting individuals from underrepresented groups in the field. I look forward to supporting you as you discover Computer Science, and I hope that through this course, you'll gain a love for Computer Science as I have. Please feel free to reach out to me with any questions, concerns, or just to chat!

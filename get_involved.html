Students

Are you a high school, college, or graduate student interested in learning more about computer science or meeting other women in technology? We would love to connect with you! Click here to sign up for our mailing list to get updates about Girls Encoded events.

Companies

Are you a company interested in supporting women in computer science? We would love for you to partner with us and help us to inspire and encourage more women in technology. There are many ways that you can be involved, from sponsoring a specific event to sending students to conferences. You can choose to target your sponsorship toward recruitment or retention (or both, if you prefer).

We are interested in working with you to find an exciting opportunity for you to be involved in the CS community here at U of M. Please get in touch with us at girls-encoded@umich.edu to discuss sponsorship.

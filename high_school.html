Girls Encoded High School Event

Girls Encoded is an exciting all-day event designed to educate and encourage girls to study computer science. The day will be filled with hands-on activities, panel discussions, and U of M CSE lab tours. This is the perfect opportunity for any girl wanting to learn more about the exciting things computer science has to offer. All high school students along with their parents are welcome. Join us Saturday, April 9th for this unique experience!
